Employee Attrition Analysis

Why Do Employees Leave Their Jobs?

This dashboard explores employee attrition as a multi-factor story. We start from job roles, compare individual employee patterns, and then trace possible pathways behind stay-or-leave outcomes.

Key Insight

Attrition is not explained by one variable alone. Higher-risk groups often appear through a combination of role, income level, overtime, and satisfaction.

Step 1

Where is attrition highest?

The bar chart compares attrition rates across job roles. Hover over a bar to see how many employees left and highlight the same role in the scatter plot.

Step 2

Do income and satisfaction explain it?

Each point represents one employee. The scatter plot connects role-level attrition patterns with individual income and satisfaction.

Step 3

Attrition is a pathway, not a single cause

The Sankey diagram shows how employees move from department to income level, overtime status, satisfaction level, and finally stayed or left.
